=== Cooking 1 ===

Cooked gudgeon (cooked_gudgeon) (gudgeon: 1)
Restores 8HP at the start of the turn if the player has lost more than 50% of his health points.

Cooked chicken (cooked_chicken) (raw_chicken: 1)
Restores 10HP at the start of the turn if the player has lost more than 50% of his health points.

=== Cooking 5 ===

Cooked beef (cooked_beef) (raw_beef: 1)
Gives 40 HP at the start of the fight
